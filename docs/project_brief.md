Business question: Which retail banking customers generate the most profit, and which are at risk of churning?

KPIs: Revenue per customer, CLV score, RFM segment distribution, churn risk rate

Scope: 45,211 customer records, calendar period implied by campaign data, Excel as primary tool

Assumptions: Balance field used as proxy for revenue; no transaction-level data available (to be simulated in Phase 2)

Success criteria: Actionable segmentation model with at least 3 executive recommendations
